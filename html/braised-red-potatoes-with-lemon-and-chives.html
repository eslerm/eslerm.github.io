Braised Red Potatoes with Lemon and Chives

Ingredients:
  • Red potatoes, 1 1/2 lb
  • Garlic, 3 cloves
  • Water, 2 cups
  • Butter, 3 tbsp
  • Thyme, 3 sprigs
  • Chives, 2 tbsp
  • Lemon juice, 1 tsp
  • Salt, 3/4 tsp
  • Pepper, 1/4 tsp
Steps:
  1. Arrange potatoes, halved side down, in 12 in skillet. Add water, butter, thyme and salt. Bring to boil and reduce to a simmer. Cover until potatoes are tender (~15 minutes).
  2. Discard thyme. Set garlic aside. Increase heat to medium high and evaporate water. Fry in butter for 15-20 minutes. Swirl pan until potatoes are brown.
  3. Meanwhile, crush and mince garlic. In a small bowl mix garlic, chives, lemon, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour potatoes into a large bowl and coat with garlic mixture
