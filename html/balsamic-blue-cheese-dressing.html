Balsamic Blue Cheese Dressing

Ingredients:
  • Balsamic vinegar, 1/4 cup
  • Olive oil, 1/2 cup
  • Salt, 1/4 tsp
  • Black pepper, 1/8 tsp
  • Hot sauce, a dash
  • Blue cheese, 1/2 cup
Steps:
  1. In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients, except for cheese, with a metal whisk.
  2. Slowly add blue cheese while whisking. Leave some cheese chunks.
Notes:
  • Salad goes well with walnuts, cranberries, and cooled rotisserie chicken.
  • Alternatively, begin by frying 3 cloves of crushed and minced garlic in hot olive oil for 15 seconds. Cool. Blend all ingredients, except for cheese, in a food processor. Add blue cheese and blend to desired consistency.
