Mediterranean Lentil Soup

Ingredients:
  • Lentils, 2 1/2 cups
  • Water, 6 cups
  • Onion, 1 large
  • Olive oil, 1 tbsp
  • Bell pepper, 1
  • Salt, 1 tsp
  • Pepper, 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin, 1 tsp
  • Cilantro, 1 cup
  • Garlic, 3 cloves
  • Lime juice, 1/4 cup
Steps:
  1. Combine lentils and water. Bring to boil. Simmer.
  2. Saute onions in half the oil. After 4 minutes, add bell peppers for 4 more minutes. Add to soup and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Season. Simmer for 15 more minutes.
  4. Stir in cilantro. Remove from heat.
  5. Blend garlic, lemon juice, and remaining oil. Add to soup.
