Crispy Sesame Tofu

Ingredients:
  • Extra firm tofu, 14 oz
  • Corn starch, 1/4 cup
  • Canola oil, enough for frying
  • Sesame seeds, 1/2 cup
  • Honey, 1/3 cup
  • Tamari sauce, 3 tbsp
  • Ginger, 3 tbsp
  • Sesame oil, 2 tbsp
  • Rice wine vinegar, 2 tbsp
  • Garlic, 2 cloves
  • Red chili pepper flakes, 2 tsp
Steps:
  1. Press tofu (~20 minutes).
  2. Combine all other ingredients, except for cornstarch. Bring this sauce to a simmer while tofu presses.
  3. Dry tofu with paper towel and dust with cornstarch.
  4. Heat 1" oil in deep frying pan until oil.
  5. Fry tofu to a golden brown.
  6. In a bowl with 2/3 cup of sauce, stir tofu.
Notes:
  • Can be served over rice with vegetables and remaining sauce.
