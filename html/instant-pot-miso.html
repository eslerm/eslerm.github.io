Instant Pot Miso

Yields: 6 servings

Ingredients:
  • Stewing chicken, 2.5 lbs
  • Ginger, 1/2 in
  • Leek, 1
  • Sweet onion, 1 large
  • Garlic, 1 head
  • Shiitake mushroom, 8
  • Bonito flakes, 1/2 cups
  • Kombu, 1 sheet
  • Sesame paste, 1 tbsp
  • White miso, 1/3 cup
  • Red Miso, 1/3 cup
  • Water, 3 quarts
  • Salt, to taste
Steps:
  1. Cut leek in half and wash thoroughly. Cut it into 1-inch pieces. Wash the chicken, and cut into large pieces.
  2. In Instant Pot, saute ginger, onions, and leak for ~10 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and fry for 15 seconds before adding water. Add shiitake mushrooms, chicken, and bonito flakes. Cover with the lid. Set to cook for 60 minutes on high heat and allow natural release.
  4. Submerge the kombu into the broth for 10 minutes. Cover.
  5. Strain the broth using a colander. In a large saucepan, add the broth and bring it to a boil. Add white miso, red miso, and sesame paste (optional). Mix well. Add salt to taste.
  6. In a bowl with ramen noodles, add miso broth, and top.
