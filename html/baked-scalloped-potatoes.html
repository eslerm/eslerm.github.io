Baked Scalloped Potatoes

Ingredients:
  • Potatoes, 6 large
  • Condensed cream of mushroom soup, 10.5 oz
  • Milk, 1 1/4 cup
  • Onion, 1 large
  • Black pepper, 1/2 tsp
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 375 F. Grease 2 qt casserole dish.
  2. Combine ingredients.
  3. Cover dish and bake for 60 minutes. Stir half-way through.
