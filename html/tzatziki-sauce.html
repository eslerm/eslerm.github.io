Tzatziki Sauce

Ingredients:
  • Plain yogurt, 2 cups
  • Cucumber, 1 large
  • Garlic, 2 cloves
  • Olive oil, 2 tbsp
  • White wine vinegar, 1 tbsp
  • Salt and Ground pepper, to taste
Steps:
  1. Double line a strainer with a cheesecloth or coffee filter. Set over a bowl. Spoon in yogurt and cover with wax paper. Allow yogurt to drain in fridge overnight.
  2. Fold in all ingredients. Refrigerate for at least one hour to blend.
Notes:
  • Serve with vegetables or pita bread.
