Oatmeal Pancakes

Ingredients:
  • Dry oatmeal, 2 cups
  • Buttermilk, 2 cups
  • Eggs, 2 medium
  • Oil, 2 tbsp
  • Flour, 1/2 cup
  • Honey, 2 tbsp
  • Baking powder, 1 tsp
  • Baking soda, 1 tsp
  • Salt, to taste
Steps:
  1. Combine the dry oatmeal and buttermilk and refrigerate overnight.
  2. Complete batter by whisking all ingredients together.
