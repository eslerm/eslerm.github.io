Chicken Wings Andalouse

Ingredients:
  • Vegetable oil, 2 tbsp
  • Chicken wings, 2 lbs
  • Red bell pepper, 1 large
  • Dry white wine, 1/4 cup
  • White wine vinegar, 1 tbsp
  • Butter, 8 tbsp
  • Tabasco sauce, 1 tbsp
  • Salt, to taste
Steps:
  1. Heat oil in large skillet over high heat. Add chicken wings and saute, turning frequently until brown and cooked through (~15-20 minutes). Dry on paper towel.
  2. Meanwhile, roast red pepper under broiler or gas flame until skin blackened and blistered. Place pepper in paper bag and steam for ~15-20 minutes. Remove skin and puree.
  3. Deglaze large skillet with win and wine vinegar. Reduce by half at high heat (2 minutes). Reduce heat to medium low and add butter 1 tbsp at a time. Whisk. Add pepper puree.
  4. Mound wings on platter and pour sauce.
