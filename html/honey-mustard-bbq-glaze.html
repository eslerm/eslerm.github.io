Honey-Mustard BBQ Glaze

Yields: 6 servings

Ingredients:
  • Applie Cider Vinegar, 1/2 cup
  • Light Brown Sugar, 2 tbsp
  • Honey, 1/3 cup
  • Djon mustard, 3 tbsp
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste
Steps:
  1. Combine vinegar and brown sugar in small saucepan over high heat. Cook for 5 minutes stirring occasionally unitl sugar is disolved.
  2. Remove from heat and whisk in other ingredients.
