Lentil Taco Filling

Ingredients:
  • Canola oil, 1 tbsp
  • Onion, 2/3 cup Finely chopped.
  • Garlic, 2 cloves Crushed and minced.
  • Lentils, 2/3 cup
  • Taco seasoning, 1 tbsp
  • Cayenne pepper, to taste
  • Chicken broth, 1 2/3 cup
Steps:
  1. Heat oil in skillet over medium heat. Cook onions until tender. Add lentils and seasonings and stir constantly for 1 minute. Make space in center of pan and fry garlic until aromatic.
  2. Add chicken broth, cover and reduce to simmer until lentils are tender (~25-30 minutes)
  3. Uncover and thicken (~6-8 minutes). Mash lentils slightly.
Notes:
  • A versatile filling. e.g., Replace seasoning with garam masala and serve with fried potatoes.
  • Great with avacado slices, pico de gallo, fajitas, and corn tortillas.
