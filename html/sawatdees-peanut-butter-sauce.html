Sawatdee's Peanut Butter Sauce

Ingredients:
  • Red chili paste, 1 tbsp
  • Coconut milk, 1 cup
  • Fish sauce, 1 tbsp
  • Crunch peanut butter, 1/2 cup
  • Sugar, 1 tbsp
Steps:
  1. In saucepan combine chili paste, coconut oil, fish sauce, & peanut butter. Bring to boil.
  2. Add sugar and boil for 2 minutes. Remove from heat.
Notes:
  • Refridgerator friendly.
