French Toast

Yields: 4 servings

Ingredients:
  • Milk, 1/2 cup
  • Heavy Cream, 1/2 cup
  • Eggs, 3 large
  • Honey, 2 tbsp Warmed.
  • Salt, 1/4 tsp
  • Stale bread, 8 0.5" slices
  • Butter, 4 tbsp
Steps:
  1. The night before, set out bread to dry and combine milk, heavy cream, eggs & butter to make custard. Refrigerate custard over night.
  2. In the morning, preheat oven to 375 F.
  3. Dip bread in custard for 30 seconds on either side. Set aside for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Over medium-high heat, melt 1 tbsp of butter in 10" pan. Place 2 slices in pan for ~2-3 minutes per side. Place in oven for 5 minutes.
Notes:
  • Sourdough bread does well.
