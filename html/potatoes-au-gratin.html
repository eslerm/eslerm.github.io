Potatoes au Gratin

Yields: 4 servings

Ingredients:
  • Russet Potatoes, 4
  • Butter, 2 tbsp Softened.
  • Heavy Cream, 3/4 cups
  • Whole Milk, 3/4 cups
  • Flour, 2 tbsp
  • Garlic, 4 cloves Finely minced.
  • Salt, 1 tsp
  • Ground pepper, to taste
  • Sharp Cheddar Cheese, 1 cup Freshly grated.
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Smear softened butter on baking dish.
  3. Slice potatoes into 1/4 inch, then cut slices into fourths.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together cream, milk, flour, minced garlic, salt, and freshly ground black pepper.
  5. Place 1/3 of the potatoes in the bottom of the baking dish. Pour 1/3 of the cream mixture over the potatoes. Repeat twice.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake until potatoes are golden brown and bubbling (~20 minutes). Add grated cheese to the top of the potatoes and bake for 3 to 5 more minute.
  7. Allow to stand for a few minutes before serving by the spoonful.
