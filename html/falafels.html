Falafels

Ingredients:
  • Dried chickpeas, 1 lb
  • Garlic, 3 cloves
  • Onion, 1 large
  • Ground coriander seeds, 1 tsp
  • Ground cumin, 1 tsp
  • Salt, 1 tsp
  • Baking soda, 1 tsp
  • Pita bread, 7 pieces
Steps:
  1. In food processor, blend chickpeas. Add all other ingrdients and mix.
  2. To deep fry, heat oil to 375F. Use a 1 1/2 inch mellon baller to form balls.
Notes:
  • Freezes well.
  • Can also be baked.
