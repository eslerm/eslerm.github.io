Pan Fried Broccoli

Ingredients:
  • Water, 3 tbsp
  • Salt, 1/4 tsp
  • Cayenne pepper, a pinch
  • Vegetable oil, 1 tbsp
  • Broccoli, 1 large head
Steps:
  1. Combine water, salt, and cayenne pepper in a small bowl.
  2. Heat oil over medium heat in large pan.
  3. Remove florets from stalk of broccoli and set aside. Remove rough edges and fully peel. Cut peeled talk in half. Slice crosswise in 1/4 inch pieces.
  4. Add stalks to hot pan for 2 minutes. Do not stir.
  5. Meanwhile, cut florets into quarters 1 1/2 inch each.
  6. After frying stalks for 2 minutes, add florets and stir. Fry for 1-2 minutes. Do not stir.
  7. Mix water bowl and add to dish and cover for 2 minutes.
  8. Remove cover, stir, and for to evaporate excess liquid before serving.
Notes:
  • 2 medium broccoli heads for a large one. Easy to scale if pan is large enough.
  • The stalk's husk is very fibrous and unpleasant to eat.
  • Florets should become a brighter green and slightly fried when finished cooking.
  • Great with jasmine rice, other vegetables, and sauces.
