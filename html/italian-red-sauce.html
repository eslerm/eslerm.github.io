Italian Red Sauce

Yields: 6 servings

Ingredients:
  • Pancetta, 6 oz
  • Spicy Italian Sausage, 1 lb
  • Meatballs, 6 large
  • Onion, 1 large
  • Carrot, 1 small
  • Garlic, 3 cloves
  • Tomato Paste, 8 oz
  • Red Wine, 1/4 cup
  • Tomatoes, 32 oz
Steps:
  1. In large pan, render fat from pancetta. Set meat aside.
  2. Cook sausage over medium high heat until browned in fat. Set meat aside.
  3. Saute onion, with carrots, until just translucent. Add garlic and fry until aromatic.
  4. Add tomato paste and spices to pan and stir for several minutes to brown.
  5. Deglaze pan with wine.
  6. Add crushed or fresh tomoatoes. Bring to simmer while stirring to desired consistency.
  7. Cut sausage to bite sizes and add meats.
  8. Simmer for 1.5 - 2 hours.
Notes:
  • Pancetta can be substituted with bacon.
  • If not using pancetta or bacon, replace fat with 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  • If not using carrot, add 1 tbsp of sugar with tomatoe sauce.
  • Add dry or fresh basil, oregano, thyme, or parsley with tomato paste as desired.
