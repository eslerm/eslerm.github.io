Garlic Sauce

Yields: half cup yield

Ingredients:
  • Rice vinegar, 2 tbsp
  • Granulated sugar, 2 tbsp
  • Light soy sauce, 1 tbsp
  • Dark soy sauce, 1 tbsp
  • Chinese rice wine, 2 tsp
  • Chili sauce, 1/4 or 1/2 tsp
  • Asian sesame oil, 1/4 tsp
  • Cornstarch, 1 1/2 tsp
  • Water, 1 tbsp
  • Peanut oil, 1 tbsp
  • Garlic, 4 cloves
Steps:
  1. Add cornstarch and water in a bowl. Keep a fork nearby to mix it later.
  2. Combine all other ingredients except for garlic and peanut oil. Mix well.
  3. Heat oil over medium heat. Add garlic and stir until aromatic.
  4. Add sauce to pan and bring to boil while stirring (~1 minute).
  5. Stir cornstarch-water mixture and add to sauce. Stir and cook to desired thickness.
Notes:
  • Batch lasts for ~6 stir fries.
