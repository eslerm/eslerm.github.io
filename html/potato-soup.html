Potato Soup

Ingredients:
  • Onion, 1 Diced.
  • Carrots, 3 Diced.
  • Celery, 3 stalks Diced.
  • Russet potatoes, 6 medium Diced.
  • Garlic, 2 cloves
  • Chicken broth, 8 cups
  • AP flour, 3 tbsp
  • Milk, 1 cup
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
Steps:
  1. Cook onions, carrots, and celery in frying pan for a few minutes. Add potatoes and fry for five more minutes.
  2. Add garlic, stir and fry until aromatic. Add broth, salt, and pepper. Bring to boil for 10 minutes. Then add milk and whisk in AP flour.
  3. Blend 2/3rds of soup and recombine.
Notes:
  • Optionally add Cajun or other spices.
