Elotes

Yields: 6 servings

Ingredients:
  • Fresh sweet corn, 6 ears
  • Mexian Crema, 3 tbsp
  • Cotija cheese, 1/2 cup
  • Chile powder, as needed
Steps:
  1. With medium-high flame grill corn, turning occasionally, until lightly charred (7-10 minutes).
  2. With pastry brush, slather corn with crema or mayo.
  3. Roll corn in cotija cheese.
  4. Powder corn with spice, as desired.
  5. Wrap base of corn stalk with paper towel.
  6. Optionally, serve with lime or cilantro clippings.
Notes:
  • El Burrito Meracado substitutes chile powder with the Pico de Gallo powder from the Pico de Gallo's brand.
