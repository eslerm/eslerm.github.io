Paneer

Yields: 12 servings

Ingredients:
  • Milk, 8 cups
  • Lemon juice, 1/4 cup
Steps:
  1. Bring milk to the boil, stirring occasionally to prevent a skin forming on top.
  2. As the milk begins to rise in the pan, stir in lemon juice in the portion of 1 tablespoon to 2 1/2 cups milk.
  3. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Soft curds should separate from the light yellow whey.
  4. Strain through muslin. Let hang for at least 30 minutes, then press to remove as much moisture as possible.
  5. If it has to be very firm, weigh down and leave for some hours in a cool place. This is necessary when it is cut into cubes and cooked with vegetable dishes, such as in palak paneer.
