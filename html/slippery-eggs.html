Slippery Eggs

Yields: 2 servings

Ingredients:
  • Shrimp, 200 grams
  • Salt, 1/4 tsp
  • Sugar, 1/4 tsp
  • White pepper powder, a pinch
  • Shaoxing wine, 1/4 tsp
  • Peanut oil, 1/2 tsp
  • Eggs, 5 medium
  • Salt, 1 tsp
  • Sugar, 1 tsp
  • Cornstarch, 1 tsp
  • Shaoxing wine, 1/2 tsp
  • MSG, sprinkle
  • Lard, 2 tsp
  • Sesame oil, 1 tsp
  • Scallions, 5 grams
  • Lard, 3 tbsp
Steps:
  1. Peel/devein shrimp. Rine shrimp under water for ~15 minutes.
  2. Thoroughly dry shrimp. More dry = more crunch.
  3. Combine shrump with marinade ingrdients sans oil. Mix well. Coat with oil. Marinate for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir fry shrimp in a wok with ~3-4 tbsp of oil. Strain.
  5. Crack eggs and add seasoning sans-oil and lard. Beat well until slightly frothy. Add in lard, sesame oil, shrimp and scallions.
  6. Scramble. Get wok hot, turn off heat, add remaing 3 tbsp of lard, swirl, heat back on, add egg mixture. Take wok on and off heat, moving cooked eggs from bottom of wok to sides before returnin to heat.
