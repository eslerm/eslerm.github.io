Dutch Baby

Yields: 2 - 4 servings

Ingredients:
  • Eggs, 3 large
  • Milk, 2/3 cup
  • Flour, 1/2 cup
  • Vanilla Extract, 1/4 tsp
  • Salt, 1/2 tsp
  • Clarified butter, 3 tbsp
  • Meyer Lemon, 1/2
  • Powdered Sugar, to taste
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 425 F.
  2. Preheat medium cast iron pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Combine all ingredients, except butter and garnish, in blender. Blend until smooth. Scrape down sides of blender halfway through.
  4. Add butter to heated pan. Pour in batter. Carefully transfer to oven.
  5. Bake at 425 F for 20-25 minutes.
  6. Brush on additional melted butter and add garnish.
Notes:
  • Fresh berries or anything that goes well with crepes make great garnishes.
