Quinoa Pilaf

Ingredients:
  • Extra virgin olive oil, 1 tbsp
  • Red onion, 1 small
  • Quinoa, 1 cup
  • Chicken broth, 2 cups
  • Salt, 1/2 tsp
  • Dried cranberries, 2/3 cups
  • Almonds, 2/3 cups
Steps:
  1. Heat oil in medium pot. Fry onions until soften (2-3 minutes).
  2. Add quinoa and toast. Stir constantly for 1 minute.
  3. Add broth and salt. Reduce heat to simmer. Cover for 10 minutes.
  4. Add cranberries and cook until quinoa is tender (8-10 minutes).
  5. Toss with almonds and serve.
