Alton Brownies

Yields: 16 Brownies

Ingredients:
  • Eggs, 4 large
  • Granulated sugar, 200 grams
  • Brown sugar, 170 grams
  • Natural Coca Powder, 70 grams
  • All-purpose flour, 70 grams
  • Kosher salt, 1/2 tsp
  • Unsalted butter, 2 sticks
  • Vanilla extract, 2 tsp
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 300F. Line an 8 inch square baking pan with parchment paper and grease.
  2. Whisk eggs until fluffy and light yellow (~2-3 minutes).
  3. Mix dry ingredients. Reduce mixing speed and slowly add to egg mixture. Add butter, 1 tbsp at a time. Mix until smooth.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes and then remove to cool for 15 minutes. Then bake until thermometer reads 195F (~30 minutes). Let cool for 30 minutes.
Notes:
  • Cut parchment paper so it folds into a box which can be pulled out.
  • Use a metal pan instead of a glass one.
